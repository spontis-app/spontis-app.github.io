About SPONTIS

SPONTIS is a living guide to Bergen’s culture and nightlife. We scrape trusted venues every day, normalise timezones and clean duplicates so you can simply pick something fun.

  • Filters tuned for spontaneous plans on mobile or desktop.
  • Time-aware curation that hides events once they are over.
