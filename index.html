    About SPONTIS

    SPONTIS is a simple weekly snapshot of what’s on in Bergen. We collect public schedules from trusted organisers, normalise timezones and remove duplicates so the feed above stays focused on what’s next.

    Everything runs as open data on GitHub Pages. Tap any listing to jump straight to the organiser for tickets or more details.
